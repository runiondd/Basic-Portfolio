About Me

I was born in Ohio and went to a very small high school in Amish country. Undergraduate in MIS from Bowling Green State University, and an MBA from Ohio State University. I started my career as a web developer coding classic ASP and javaScript then moving to C# and ASP.NET. I have a dog, am passionate about health & fitness, and have a healthy addition to anything with wheels. At one time I owned 4 motorcycles, but I've got that down to 1 and a half (it's a long term project).
